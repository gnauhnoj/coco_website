About

What We're Trying to Achieve

Physical inactivity, common in 1 out of 4 adults, has been identified as the fourth leading risk factor for global mortality. We propose that by adding physically-interactive storytelling, we can make digital technology the solution, rather than the problem. Stories That Move is an Augmented Reality (AR)-based story platform that will engage the user with a story, while simultaneously requiring the user to actively explore the physical world in order to move the story forward.



Visit our GitHub

Tasks

Our Platform is based on the Fundamental Tasks

Traversal

Users will need to traverse the physical environment while using the interface to move the story forward.

Knowledge Acquisition

Users will need to listen to and watch the characters interact with their physical environment using context clues to extract information.

Interaction

Users will need to manipulate items in their physical and virtual environment to complete in-story tasks.
